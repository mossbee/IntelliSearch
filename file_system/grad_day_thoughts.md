# June 15, 2024 - I graduated!

Four years have passed in the blink of an eye.

The weather is wonderful today, with bright sunshine. When the principal called my name, my mind went blank.
Wearing this pink sash (Stole), it feels so real that I am finally a CUHK graduate.

The happiest thing was that both my parents and my boyfriend were present. Mom seemed even more excited than I was in the audience.

I had a huge amount of xiaolongbao at Din Tai Fung in the evening. It felt like the most relaxing meal in the past four years.

Goodbye, Hong Kong.
The next stop is Singapore! NTU, here I come!
